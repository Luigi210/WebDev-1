Oatmel!

  • Time: 15min
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Oatmel 200g
Water 150ml
Sugar 100g

Step 1: Boil water.

Step 2: Add oatmel.

Step 3: Add sugar and mix.

Step 4: Wait 10-15min.

Step 5: Ready!

Pasta!

  • Time: 35min
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Pasta 200g
Water 300ml
Cheese 100g
Milk 100ml

Step 1: Boil water.

Step 2: Add pasta.

Step 3: Heat oil on a frying pan.

Step 4: Add milk and wait 2-3min.

Step 5: Add cheese and mix.

Step 6: Mix pasta and sauce.

Step 7: Ready!

Source: Youtube video!
